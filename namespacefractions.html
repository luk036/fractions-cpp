Computes the least common multiple of two integers.

Uses the formula lcm(a, b) = (abs(a) / gcd(a, b)) * abs(b).

Template Parameters
MnThe integer type.
Parameters
_mThe first integer.
_nThe second integer.
Returns
The least common multiple of _m and _n.
*    lcm(4, 6) = 12
*
*    4 = 2^2
*    6 = 2^1 * 3^1
*    lcm = 2^2 * 3^1 = 12
*

*    |  4  |  lcm  |  6  |  =   |   12   |
